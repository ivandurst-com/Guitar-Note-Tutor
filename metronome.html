Need Help?

This exercise is intended to help you learn the notes on the guitar, one string at a time. Pick a string to start with, set your beats per minute and your beats per bar, and click "Start". You will be shown a note - try to play that note on your chosen string on the 4th beat of the measure! Once you get good at it, try to play it on the 3rd, then 2nd beat of the measure, then move to the next string when you're ready. Good luck!
